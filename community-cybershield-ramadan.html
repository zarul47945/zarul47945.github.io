Cybershield Ramadan.

FISE CSR program at Kampung Olak Lempit on 7 March 2026, combining Ramadan community service, online-scam awareness and Barakah Pantry distribution.

Activity summary

7 Mar 2026
Kampung Olak Lempit

Participant - FISE CSR Program

The program supported asnaf families during Ramadan while sharing practical knowledge about online scams and simple digital income ideas such as TikTok and Shopee affiliate concepts.

  • Supported community engagement and helped the program flow during the CSR activity.
  • Joined knowledge sharing around scam awareness and safe online behavior.
  • Participated in house-to-house Barakah Pantry distribution for selected families.

Reflection notes

Objective

Serve and educate

The activity combined donation support with digital safety awareness, making the program useful both socially and practically.

What I learned

Community sensitivity

I learned how to explain digital topics in a simple, respectful way for a mixed audience while staying attentive to community needs.

Challenge

Clear communication

The team had to manage technical explanations and multiple house visits through patience, task sharing and cooperation.
