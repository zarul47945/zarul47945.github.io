Hearts for Hope.

CSR orphanage fundraising program for Rumah Amal Budi Bistari, with fundraising from 22 July 2024 to 2 August 2024 at MSU Campus.

Activity summary

22 Jul - 2 Aug 2024
Rumah Amal Budi Bistari

Assistant Program Manager

The program raised funds and collected useful contributions for orphanage children through donation campaigns, food and drink sales, sponsorship efforts and promotional work.

  • Supported the Head of Activity and Logistics teams.
  • Helped prepare activity materials, logistics needs, transport and team readiness.
  • Worked with multiple committees to keep fundraising and program preparation coordinated.

Reflection notes

Objective

Support children

The activity focused on raising funds and collecting essential items while promoting compassion, responsibility and collaboration among students.

What I learned

Leadership support

My role strengthened planning, coordination, accountability and cross-team communication across activity and logistics work.

Challenge

Many moving parts

The team managed sponsorship progress, materials, transport and member coordination through meetings, delegation and follow-up discussions.
