## Recommended Media

- Images: JPG, landscape where possible, at least 1600 pixels wide.
- Portrait: vertical JPG, ideally 4:5 ratio.
- Videos: MP4 (H.264), 1080p or 720p, preferably under 90 seconds.
- Avoid screenshots containing passwords, private student data, or personal IDs.
